Prompt used for the monolith transformation

"Cinematic surreal desert transformation. Start from the image of the dark translucent monolith standing alone in the desert at sunset. Keep the same camera angle, same lighting, same color palette, same desert environment and same monolith design. The wind begins to intensify around the base of the monolith. Sand starts moving in circular patterns, forming a slow but powerful vortex around the structure. The sand rises from the dunes in elegant spirals, as if being magnetically pulled by the monolith. Gradually, the swirling sand gathers on one side of the monolith and begins to sculpt the shape of a giant human head emerging from the desert, matching the reference image: closed eyes, serene expression, monumental scale, made entirely of sand and dust. The face should feel ancient, calm and mystical, partially fused with the dunes. Once the head is fully formed, it slowly lifts upward from the desert, still made of sand particles. The head rises toward the sky in a weightless, dreamlike motion, passing beside the monolith. As it ascends, the edges begin to dissolve into fine golden dust. The head then gradually disintegrates into thousands of sand particles carried by the wind. The particles spread into the cloudy sky, then slowly fall back down like a soft sand rain over the desert dunes. End with the monolith standing again in the quiet desert, surrounded by falling dust and fading wind. Slow cinematic motion, atmospheric, mystical, photorealistic, high detail, volumetric dust, soft sunset light, elegant particle simulation, no new objects, no text, no people, no camera cuts, smooth continuous transition."

Negative prompt

"Do not change the monolith shape, do not add extra buildings, do not add people, do not add animals, do not add text, do not make the face look cartoonish, do not make the movement too fast, do not change the desert location, do not use bright fantasy colors, do not create explosions, avoid horror, avoid aggressive facial expression."
